Explore Mabini's Natural Wonders

Anilao Diving Spots

Discover vibrant coral reefs and diverse marine life in one of the world's premier diving destinations.

Mt. Gulugod Baboy

Hike to breathtaking summit views overlooking Batangas Bay and the surrounding islands.

Sea Spring Resort

Explore pristine coastlines and crystal-clear waters perfect for snorkeling and island hopping.

Local Delicacies

Savor authentic Batangas cuisine and fresh seafood prepared with traditional recipes.

Culture & Local Life

Marine Conservation

Local communities work together to preserve the rich marine biodiversity that makes Anilao famous worldwide.

Culinary Heritage

Traditional cooking methods and family recipes passed down through generations create unforgettable flavors.

Island Traditions

Experience the warm hospitality and vibrant festivals that celebrate Mabini's coastal heritage.
